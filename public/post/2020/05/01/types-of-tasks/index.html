Types of tasks

2020/05/01

1min read

In my work so far, there are typically a few type of tasks:

  • Analytics tasks (planning, architecture, setup, coding etc)
  • Writing (documentations, reflections)
  • Desktop research
  • Emails (replying, drafting new ones)
  • Ideations
  • Team huddles
  • External meetings with customers
  • Internal project meetings
  • Preparing decks and presentations
  • Organising materials
  • Admin

I think they can divided into four quadrants:

High amount of visible results, low value * Emails (replying, drafting new ones) * Organising materials High amount of visible results, high value * Analytics tasks (coding, planning) * Writing * Preparing decks and presentations * Customer meetings
Low amount of visible results, low value * Admin * Internal project meetings Low amount of visible results, high value * Desktop research * Ideations, brainstorming * Team huddles
